# Bharat Petroleum Corporation Ltd. (BPCL) (2008–2009)

## Industrial Systems, Asset Governance & Reliability Engineering

BPCL provided Rajesh Arigala with foundational experience in operating and governing mission-critical industrial systems within a large-scale refinery environment.

Working across maintenance, reliability, inspections, turnarounds, budgeting, contracts, materials planning, procurement, SAP workflows, and cross-functional operations, the role required coordinating multiple technical and business functions to maintain safe, reliable, and economically viable refinery operations.

The refinery was approached as a systems-of-systems environment where assets, people, processes, budgets, vendors, enterprise systems, and operational objectives had to function as a unified operating model.

---

Operate and maintain critical refinery infrastructure while balancing:

- Reliability

within a 24×7 industrial production environment.

## Leadership Principle

A foundational lesson from BPCL was that complex systems rarely fail because of individual components.

They fail when coordination breaks down between people, processes, assets, budgets, vendors, and enterprise systems.

Reliability emerges when interfaces are governed effectively.

This principle later influenced Rajesh's approach to enterprise platforms, cloud-native systems, MLOps, platform engineering, and Generative AI systems.
